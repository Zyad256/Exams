Course Syllabus

Exams strictly focus on the core foundational concepts of the first 5 chapters:

  • Ch 1

    Reliable, Scalable, & Maintainable Applications

    Hardware/software faults, load parameters, and latency vs. throughput trade-offs.

  • Ch 2

    Data Models & Query Languages

    Comparing Relational, Document, NoSQL, and Graph schemas for complex relationships.

    Storage & Retrieval

    Diving into SSTables, Log-Structured Merge (LSM) Trees, memtables, and Bloom filters.

    Encoding & Evolution

    Data evolution systems, schema compatibility (backward/forward), JSON, and XML.

    Replication & Distributed Nodes

    Single-leader, multi-leader, leaderless systems, CAP theorem, quorums, and replication lag.
